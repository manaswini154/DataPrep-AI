FAQ

Common questions

What file formats are supported? ▼
We support CSV and Excel files (.xlsx, .xls). Files are processed in your authenticated session and are never stored permanently.
Do I need to know Python or machine learning? ▼
Not at all. DataPrep AI is designed for anyone working with tabular data — from analysts to ML engineers. Everything is visual and explained in plain English.
How does the AI feature engineering work? ▼
Our system profiles each column — checking skewness, outliers, cardinality, and column relationships — then retrieves the most relevant transformation techniques for your specific data profile.
Is my data private and secure? ▼
Yes. Datasets are processed in your authenticated session only and are not stored or shared. We use bcrypt for passwords and JWT tokens for session security.
What is Review Mode? ▼
Review mode shows every proposed change — null fills, whitespace fixes, duplicate removals, column renames — before applying them. Accept or reject each one individually.
